About Us

Welcome to SPATIALMIND GEO TECHNOLOGIES

Specializes in Photogrammetry/ LiDAR/ Automated Mapping /Facilities Management (AM/FM) and Geographic Information Systems (GIS) solutions, SGT has based its premises in Kakinada- India.

SGT has the best professionals who have the knowledge to blend the technologies of digital mapping from variety of sources including Satellite images, Aerial photographs, GPS field data and existing maps leading to the GIS database creation. We consider each and every step of the process from digitization to delivery quiet crucial and encourage SGT team to understand the process orientation and time targets. As we strictly believe in "DOING IT RIGHT FIRST TIME", we train our team at regular intervals, adequate on project essentials before and during every project. We believe in "DON'T ASSUME, ASK TO BE RIGHT", we will be continuously in touch with the quality responsible to clarify our doubts from time to time, and to produce the best quality.
